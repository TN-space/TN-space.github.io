About me

After months of hard work, I can finally call myself a full-stack software engineer. Programming is one of the most difficult, yet most interesting thing I've ever learned. It is a constant changing, ever evolving world that is so much different, and yet so close to the human world. There is no one correct answer for any problems. Take finding a book for instance, we all know how to it the human way. But if you apply the exact execution onto a machine, it won't work; instead the machine will be have to go through each book to find said book. The process could take a long time, depending on the number of books. If the books were sorted, binary search would be the best option. Search time would literally be cut in half. Amazing, isn't it? The world of programming is full of those difficult, interesting moments, and I can't wait to emerge myself in it.
